A mechanic by pressing space bar:
1. Enemies spawn in different colors
2. Depending what "color/phase/reality" is active, certain enemies will be "physical" (can be damaged) & others are transparent (can not be damaged)
3. The Player can change "color/phase/reality" by pressing space.
4. By pressing Space, the transparent enemies become "physical" & the "physical" enmemies become transparent
5. By pressing Space, the Player changes color that fits (as well as the projectile changes color to match)
